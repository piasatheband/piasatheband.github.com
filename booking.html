Booking Information

Want us to play a show? Just send an email to <booking@piasatheband.com>.

Considerations

Pricing: While we typically request $100 per hour of performance, Piasa has also played a number of door-split shows and is willing to do so in the future.

Set Length: Piasa can fill time slots of up to 4 hours in length and has no minimum time requirements.

Material: Piasa primarily focuses on original material. Covers of songs from bands such as Radiohead, Death Cab for Cutie, and Wilco (among others) often make it into the live rotation as well.

Equipment: Piasa has everything needed to put on a great show. Besides instruments, the band owns microphones, a PA system, and a sixteen channel mixer. Stage lighting has not yet been acquired.
